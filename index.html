About The Makery Project

Empowering the next generation of creators, thinkers, and innovators

Making STEAM Accessible to All

The Makery Project is a nonprofit organization dedicated to providing accessible, hands-on STEAM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts, and Mathematics) and maker-centered learning opportunities for youth, families, and educators.

Through our mobile makerspace programs and educational initiatives, we bring the power of making directly to communities that need it most. We believe that when young people learn to create with their hands and minds, they develop critical thinking skills, confidence, and a lifelong love of learning.

Leadership

Meet Our Founder

Robert D. Wachtel Pronovost, PhD

Founder & Executive Director

With extensive background in maker education, Dr. Pronovost founded The Makery Project to address the critical need for accessible STEAM education in underserved communities.

His vision is simple yet powerful: every child, regardless of background or circumstance, deserves the opportunity to discover the joy of making and develop the creative problem-solving skills that will serve them throughout their lives.
